About Summit

Welcome to the 5th edition of TechNova IT Summit, Gujarat's premier gathering for developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts. This event is engineered to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands in the ever-evolving world of technology.

At TechNova, you will witness live coding battles, hackathons, and seminars from industry leaders who are shaping the future of AI and Web 3.0. Immerse yourself in a digital ecosystem designed to upgrade your technical stack and soft skills.

Our tech-expo zone will feature the latest in robotics, VR/AR, and software solutions, giving you hands-on experience with next-gen gadgets. Network with CTOs, project managers, and fellow coders to find your next big career break.

Join us at TechNova IT Summit 2025 and compile your dreams into reality. Let's code the future together!
